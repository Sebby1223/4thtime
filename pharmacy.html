Intro to Java Medical Assisting Nursing Pharmacy

Welcome to Pharmacy!

This course is for people who are looking to study in the Medical compartment, specifically for drugs and perscriptions. In this course, you will learn the important needs of these perscriptions, their effects, side-effects, and need of use.

What is the Content of this Program?

In this program, you will learn the aspects of what a drug is, what people use it for, etc. You will be given a large textbook containing all the drugs and perscriptions you need to know and what their side-effects and effects are on the person. Also, this is a special program in which you are able to go to Walgreens and intern there for a portion of your Junior year and the Hospital for your senior year. This helps you be immersed in the pharmaceutical environment and see what it is like perscribing perscriptions to people. By the end of this program, you will...

  • Learn basic medical terminology
  • Complex understanding of certain drugs and their effects
  • How to perscribe perscriptions to patients and address their needs.

Now knowing the general gist of the course, there are also a lot of work involved into learning the material.

Throughout your junior and senior year, you will be tasked to learn many medical terms and drug names. From the textbook you are given, you are to study it frequently for the weekly tests in the course. In this program, like other medical programs, you can apply your skills in national competitions such as SkillsUSA and HOSA.
