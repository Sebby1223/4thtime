Technical Program Details

Intro to Java Medical Assisting Nursing Pharmacy

Welcome to Nursing!

This course is for people who are looking to study in the medical field specialized in direct treatment of patients and families This program introduces students in not just basical medical concepts and terminology, but also an advanced curriculum to push students to get their ceritifcation .

What is the Content of this Program?

In this program, you will be be learning many medical terms and be engaged in an environment where you can apply your skills. In the room, there are plenty of demonstrations of how to treat a patient and see what certain body parts look like. By the end of this course, you will...

  • Learn medical terminology
  • Understand symptoms of a patient and how to treat them
  • How to apply your skills in a day to day life

Now knowing the general gist of the course, there are also a lot of work involved into learning these concepts.

Throughout your junior and senior year, you will be assigned multiple tasks, such as weekly tests that you are REQUIRED to pass in order to stay in the program. In McFatter, this is usually deemed one of the hardest programs due to the risks, if you are to fail 1-2 exams, you are out. Other than that, you will be asked to do demonstrations of the techniques learned on props made specifically for the classroom and you are in a "nursing" environment. To apply the skills you've learned, there are plenty of competitions such as HOSA and SkillsUSA.
