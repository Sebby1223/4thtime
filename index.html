Medical Assisting

Practical Nursing

As a Nurse, you will learn crucial skills on taking care of patients directly and monitoring the heatlh of individuals, families, and play an essential role in the physical and emotional care of the patients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the Frequently Asked Questions About Our School!

What are the requirements to be eligible to go to this school?

Have at least a 2.5 GPA in the four core classes (English, Math, Science and Social Studies) on the most recent report card. Also, you need to obtain a Level 3 or higher in both FSA/EOC Math and Reading from the previous school year. If the FSA is not available, at least 50th percentile in Math and Reading on most nationally recognized standardized test will be accepted.

What is a Technical Program and What do you get out of it?

McFatter Specializes in these programs specified for certain fields. These fields such as Medical Assisting and Pharmacy allow you to obtain a certification by the end of your senior year as you start your junior year. These programs help students get a jumpstart on their careers and learn more information about them for the future. After completing the necessary requirements and hours, you are to obtain a form of certification indicating you have completed the program which is very useful for students future careers and individual skills.

How are the teachers here?

Amazing! From personal feedback from the team as seniors, there has not been a single teacher that did not know how to manage their classroom not only well, but in a fun way to engage the students. They are very supportive and interact with their students rather than just teaching a lesson. They are also super friendly with their tone and you can be in a comfortable learning environment with some of the most qualified teachers.

How is the school environment?

Superb! The school environment is not like any other, since we are such a small school, everyone practically has seen each other before! This can help make interactions easier and provide a more comfortable environment. Moreover, there hasn't been a single fight in years! McFatter is the type of school where not only it is a academic-heavy environment, but also an environment where there is no senseless fights over the stupidest things and people are usually very nice!

If I need Help unrelating to academics, who do I contact?

Due to the friendly environment of the school, it is also very easy to talk to the counselors as well! Faculty such as Mr. Harris and Ms. Crichlow provide excellent guidance throughout your high school years. They also are very friendly and love interacting with the students and engaging with them in school activities. The faculty are down-to-earth people and you can talk to them about anything, relating to classes or even how you feel about the school!
