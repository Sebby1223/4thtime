Welcome to Java!

This course is for people who are looking to study in the Computer Sciene department. This program introduces students to basic computer science principles and coding languages and helps students get a head start on their career.

What is the Content of this Program?

In this program, there will be a variety of languages you learn. First, starting your junior year, you will learn basic HTML and CSS which is essentially web development. After that, you will be assigned to learn other languages like javascript for your website. To help you learn web development, there is also an AP course implemented which is AP Computer Science Principles where it teaches you the basics of computer science and their terms. In your senior year, you will mostly be learning Java. By the end of this course you will

  • Learn multiple computer languages
  • Basic understanding of CS Terms
  • How to implement your knowledge into programming

Now knowing the general gist of the course, there are also a lot of work involved into learning these concepts.

Throughout your junior and senior year, you will be assigned multiple tasks, including projects where you implement your knowledge. There will be assignments on websites to help you learn as well, such as Runestone, CodeHS, Code.org, etc. In this program you will be prepared efficiently to apply your knowledge in the future and get a jump start on your path in computer science.
